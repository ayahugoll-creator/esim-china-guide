We test eSIMs for China travel. Honestly. No sponsored rankings.

## Why This Site Exists

Every traveler to China faces the same problem: **which eSIM actually works behind the Great Firewall?**

Google, WhatsApp, Instagram, Gmail — all blocked. Most eSIM providers claim to "work in China," but half of them don't bypass the firewall, and the other half throttle speeds to unusable levels.

We buy and test every eSIM with our own money, then write honest reviews.

## How We Make Money

When you click a link on this site and purchase an eSIM, we may earn a small commission — at **no extra cost to you**. We never accept payment to inflate ratings or hide negative findings.

If a provider is bad, we say so. Period.
