## What About a Chinese Phone Number?

All international eSIMs are **data-only**. No +86 number.

You only need a Chinese number if you:
- Want to use Meituan (food delivery) standalone app
- Need 12306 train ticket app (Trip.com can book trains for you)
- Register for some local services

**For 90% of tourists, data-only is enough.** Alipay works with your foreign number. DiDi works through Alipay's mini-program. You don't need a local SIM.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I install the eSIM after arriving in China?**
A: Install BEFORE departure. QR codes and activation links may not load behind the firewall.

**Q: Do these work in Hong Kong and Macau?**
A: Check the plan — "Mainland China only" plans exclude HK/Macau. Look for "Greater China" plans if visiting all three.

**Q: Can I make phone calls?**
A: No. These are data-only eSIMs. Use WhatsApp/FaceTime/WeChat for calls.

**Q: Will my phone work with eSIM?**
A: iPhones (XS/XR and newer), most Samsung Galaxy, Google Pixel 4+. Check your phone's specs.
