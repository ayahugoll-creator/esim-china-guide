## Australia → China: Visa-Free Entry in 2026

Australian passport holders are among the lucky ones. Since July 2024, Australia has been on China's unilateral visa-free list. The policy has been extended multiple times and is currently valid until **December 31, 2026**.

### What You Can Do

- Tourism (sightseeing, visiting friends)
- Business meetings and conferences
- Family visits
- Transit through China

### What You Cannot Do

- Work or paid employment
- Study (short training is OK, enrollment is not)
- Journalism without special permission
- Religious missionary activities

### Before You Fly

1. **Download Alipay** and link your Australian bank card (Visa/Mastercard works). [Full setup guide →](/posts/alipay-foreigner-setup-guide/)
2. **Get an eSIM** before departure. Install while you still have internet — QR codes may not load behind the Great Firewall. [Compare China eSIMs →](/posts/best-esim-for-china-travel/)
3. **Print your hotel booking** and return flight ticket. Border officers rarely ask, but carry them just in case.
