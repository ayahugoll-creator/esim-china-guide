## Germany → China: Visa-Free Entry in 2026

Germany was among the first European countries granted China's unilateral visa-free access. German citizens have had visa-free entry since late 2023, and the policy has been consistently renewed.

### Key Facts

- **Status:** Visa-free (unilateral policy)
- **Duration:** 30 days per visit
- **Valid until:** December 31, 2026
- **Multiple entries:** Yes — leave and re-enter with no cooldown period

### What German Travelers Should Know

- Direct flights from Frankfurt, Munich, Berlin to Beijing, Shanghai, Guangzhou, Chengdu
- Lufthansa and Air China both operate daily routes
- eSIM installation works fine on German mobile networks before departure
- Alipay accepts German Visa/Mastercard (Deutsche Bank, Commerzbank, Sparkasse — all tested working)
