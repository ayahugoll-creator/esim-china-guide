## UK → China: Visa-Free Entry in 2026

Great news for British travelers: as of **February 17, 2026**, UK citizens can enter China visa-free for up to 30 days. This was one of the most anticipated additions to the visa-free list.

### Key Facts

- **Status:** Visa-free (unilateral policy)
- **Duration:** 30 days per visit
- **Valid until:** December 31, 2026
- **Purpose:** Tourism, business, family visits, transit

### British-Specific Notes

- The policy covers **all British citizen passport holders**
- BNO passport holders should check with the Chinese embassy separately
- British Overseas Territory citizens may have different requirements
