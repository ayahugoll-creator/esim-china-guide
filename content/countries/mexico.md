## Mexico → China: Entry Requirements 2026



**Short version:** Mexico citizens are NOT on China's 30-day visa-free list, but CAN use the 240-hour (10-day) transit visa-free program if flying through China to a third country.

**The trick:** Book a multi-city itinerary: Mexico → Beijing (stay 5 days) → Tokyo/Seoul/Bangkok (onward). Your transit visa-free is valid for 10 days.
