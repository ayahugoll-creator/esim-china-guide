## India → China: Entry Requirements 2026

Indian citizens currently need a visa for all types of travel to China. India is not included in any of China's visa-free programs.

### Visa Options

| Type | Duration | Best For |
|------|----------|----------|
| L (Tourist) | 30-60 days, single/double entry | Tourism, visiting family |
| M (Business) | 30-90 days, single/double/multiple | Business meetings |
| F (Visit) | Varies | Academic exchanges, non-business visits |

### How to Apply

1. Fill the online application at the [Chinese Visa Application Service Center](https://www.visaforchina.cn/)
2. Book an appointment at the center in New Delhi, Mumbai, Kolkata, or Chennai
3. Bring all documents + printed application
4. Pay fee, receive receipt
5. Pick up passport in 4-7 business days
